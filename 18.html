Argon

Argon is a chemical element with the symbol Ar and atomic number 18. It is a colorless, odorless, inert monatomic gas under standard conditions, making up about 0.93% of the Earth's atmosphere. Argon is used in lighting, welding, and as an inert gas in various industrial processes.
