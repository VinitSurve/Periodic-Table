Mercury

Mercury is a chemical element with the symbol Hg and atomic number 80. It is a dense, silvery-white liquid metal. Mercury is the only metal that is liquid at standard conditions for temperature and pressure. Mercury is used in thermometers, barometers, and other scientific apparatus.
