Livermorium

Livermorium is a synthetic chemical element with the symbol Lv and atomic number 116. It is highly radioactive and only trace amounts have been produced in laboratories. The properties of livermorium are not well known, but it is expected to be a solid metal at room temperature and to exhibit chemical properties similar to those of polonium and flerovium.
