Aluminium

Aluminium is a chemical element with the symbol Al and atomic number 13. It is a silvery-white, soft, non-magnetic and ductile metal. Aluminium is the most abundant metal in the Earth's crust and is widely used in various applications due to its light weight, corrosion resistance, and high thermal and electrical conductivity.
