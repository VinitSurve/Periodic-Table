Tantalum

Tantalum is a chemical element with the symbol Ta and atomic number 73. It is a rare, hard, blue-gray metal that is highly corrosion-resistant. Tantalum is used in electronics, particularly in capacitors and high-power resistors, as well as in surgical implants and aerospace components.
