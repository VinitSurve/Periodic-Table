Hafnium

Hafnium is a chemical element with the symbol Hf and atomic number 72. It is a lustrous, silvery gray, tetravalent transition metal that resists corrosion and is used in nuclear control rods. Hafnium is used in superalloys and as a catalyst for the production of synthetic fibers, films, and paints.
