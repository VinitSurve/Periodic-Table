Holmium

Holmium is a chemical element with the symbol Ho and atomic number 67. It is a silvery-white, malleable and ductile metal that is part of the lanthanide series. Holmium is known for its magnetic properties and is used in various applications, including nuclear reactors and as a dopant in solid-state lasers.
