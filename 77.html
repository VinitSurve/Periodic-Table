Iridium

Iridium is a chemical element with the symbol Ir and atomic number 77. It is a very hard, brittle, silvery-white transition metal of the platinum group. Iridium is the second-densest element after osmium and is the most corrosion-resistant metal known. Iridium is used in high-performance alloys and as a catalyst.
