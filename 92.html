Uranium

Uranium is a chemical element with the symbol U and atomic number 92. It is a dense, silvery-white metal that is weakly radioactive. Uranium is used as fuel for nuclear reactors and in the manufacture of nuclear weapons. It is the heaviest naturally occurring element.
