Moscovium

Moscovium is a chemical element with the symbol Mc and atomic number 115. It is a synthetic element that is not found in nature and is highly radioactive. Moscovium is part of the p-block of the periodic table and is expected to exhibit properties similar to those of thallium, but its exact characteristics are still largely unknown due to its short half-life.
