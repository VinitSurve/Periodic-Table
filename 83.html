Bismuth

Bismuth is a chemical element with the symbol Bi and atomic number 83. It is a brittle, white metal with a slight pink tinge. Bismuth is known for its low toxicity compared to other heavy metals and is used in cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, and as a replacement for lead in various applications.
