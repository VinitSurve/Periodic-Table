Lutetium

Lutetium is a chemical element with the symbol Lu and atomic number 71. It is a silvery-white metal that is the heaviest and least abundant of the stable lanthanides. Lutetium is used in various applications, including as a catalyst in oil refining and as a dopant in optical fibers.
