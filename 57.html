Lanthanum

Lanthanum is a soft, ductile, silvery-white metallic element that tarnishes when exposed to air, forming a dull coating. Lanthanum is the eponymous member of the lanthanide series and is used in specialty glass, camera lenses, and carbon-arc lighting. It is also used as a catalyst for petroleum refining.
