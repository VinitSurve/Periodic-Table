Barium

Barium is a chemical element with the symbol Ba and atomic number 56. It is a soft, silvery-white alkaline earth metal that is highly reactive and readily forms compounds with oxygen and water. Barium is primarily used in the production of glass, ceramics, and in medical imaging.
