Nitrogen

Nitrogen is a chemical element with the symbol N and atomic number 7. It is a colorless, odorless, tasteless, and mostly inert diatomic gas at standard conditions. Nitrogen is the fifth most abundant element in the universe and the largest single component of the Earth's atmosphere. It is an essential element for all known forms of life, playing a key role in the structure of proteins, nucleic acids, and other vital molecules.
