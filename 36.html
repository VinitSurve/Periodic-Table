Krypton

Krypton is a chemical element with the symbol Kr and atomic number 36. It is a colorless, odorless, inert monatomic gas under standard conditions, with about two-thirds the density of air. Krypton is the third-lightest noble gas, and is the fifth most abundant element in the universe. It is used in signs, lighting, and other specialized applications.
