Magnesium

Magnesium is a chemical element with the symbol Mg and atomic number 12. It is a shiny gray solid that bears a close physical resemblance to its two neighbors in the periodic table, aluminum and silicon. Magnesium is the eighth most abundant element in the universe and is essential for all living cells.
