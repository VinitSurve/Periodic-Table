Rutherfordium

Rutherfordium is a synthetic chemical element with the symbol Rf and atomic number 104. It is a radioactive metal that is extremely unstable and difficult to produce in macroscopic quantities. Rutherfordium is named after Ernest Rutherford, a pioneering physicist in the study of radioactivity.
