Actinium

Actinium is a chemical element with the symbol Ac and atomic number 89. It is a soft, silvery-white, radioactive metal that glows in the dark. Actinium was discovered in 1899 by Friedrich Oskar Giesel. It is used as a neutron source and in radiation therapy for cancer treatment.
