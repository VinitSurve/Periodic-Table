Phosphorus

Phosphorus is a chemical element with the symbol P and atomic number 15. It is a non-metal that exists in several allotropes, including white, red, and black phosphorus. Phosphorus is essential for life, playing a key role in DNA, RNA, and ATP, and is used in fertilizers, detergents, and pesticides.
